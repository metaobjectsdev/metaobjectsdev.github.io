Community Guidelines

Our Commitment

The MetaObjects community is committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

Expected Behavior

  • Be respectful and inclusive in all interactions
  • Provide constructive feedback and accept criticism gracefully
  • Focus on what's best for the community and project
  • Help newcomers learn and contribute effectively
  • Follow technical best practices and coding standards

Unacceptable Behavior

  • Harassment, discrimination, or offensive language
  • Spamming or inappropriate promotional content
  • Publishing private information without consent
  • Trolling, insulting, or deliberately disruptive behavior
  • Any conduct harmful to the community
